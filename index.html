Collecting Figurines?

Collecting figurines is a hobby where people gather and keep small, detailed sculptures or models,
often based on characters from pop culture, animals, fantasy themes, or other interests.
They can be made from materials like plastic, resin, porcelain, or metal.
These figurines can be displayed for aesthetic, nostalgic, or sentimental reasons.
